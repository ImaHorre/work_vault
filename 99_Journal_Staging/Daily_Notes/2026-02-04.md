## General
- Hot embossing experimentation via alternative methods

## Testing
- **Laminator roller hot embossing**: Temp set to max 160°C
- **Material stack testing**: Various combinations with PET sandwich
- **Best results**: 100% ECOC + 100% F600 backing (stuck together nicely)
- **Issues identified**: Trapped air between layers, air trapped in E100 layer
- **Process**: Preheat at low pressure, back and forth motion (no one-shot 6P)

## Observations
- Hand-rolled E100 on shim vs straight laminator: way more trapped air on direct laminator sample
- Trapped microbubbles in polymer (not from shim contact)
- Hot press possibly running too hot for F600

## Follow-up
- **Process improvements to test**:
  - Roll at slightly higher temp with elevated pressure
  - Preheat stack on hot plate
  - Preheat plastic only, then roll into shim
  - Layer E100 first, then F600 to minimize trapped air
- **V4 device testing**: V4DN22 showed mixed results - some normal, some abnormal snap-off

## General
- Shim processing and UV device testing

## Testing
- 1um UV sealed at 90c 3P (no anneal process)
- Had flow through DFUS (water) via syringe pump flow  
- Burst over DFUS at 500mbar oil, 2 hours into filling
- Very easy to peel off delaminated area

## Observations
- UV sealing at 90c without annealing shows flow capability
- 500mbar oil pressure threshold for 1um devices
- Delamination occurs easily after burst - indicates weak bonding
- Water flow successful, oil pressure causes failure

## Follow-up  
- Shims processed: 1um, 30um x2, 10um into 1st casts
- Made 2nd cast tests for water filling/annealing trials
- 1um likely needs high pressure (1000-1500 mbar) for DFU propagation

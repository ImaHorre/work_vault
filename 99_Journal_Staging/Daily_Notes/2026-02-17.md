## Testing
- **Imprint method for 1um structures**: 
  - Previous attempts: ramping temp but not changing pressure much
  - Result: Damaged/bent small microchannel structures
  - **New approach**: Preheat with no pressure to prevent structural damage

## Observations
- Current hot emboss approach damages delicate 1um structures
- Temperature ramping without pressure control causes deformation
- Need more careful pressure control for fine features

## Follow-up
- **Process development**: Implement preheat with no pressure approach
- **Parameter optimization**: Develop pressure profiles for different feature sizes
- **Equipment evaluation**: Assess if current hot emboss setup suitable for 1um

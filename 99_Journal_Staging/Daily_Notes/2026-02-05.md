## General

## Testing
- **Bond test**: E100/F600 imprint bond failed
- **Results**: Majority fine with some edge delamination
- **Observation**: Peel-off causes surface damage but bond is strong
- **Hot press method**: Preheating helps with F600 imprinting
- **Improved process**: Laminate COC between PET first → heat in press → peel PET → apply shim + pressure roll

## Observations
- Strong bond achieved but edge delamination still occurs
- Preheating significantly improves imprint quality
- Multi-step process reduces trapped air bubbles
- Surface damage indicates bond strength adequate

## Follow-up
- **Repeat testing**: Multiple bond attempts with varied parameters
- **Parameter optimization**: Lower temps, different times
- **Process refinement**: Apply improved multi-step method to E100
